Canvas City Infrequently Asked Questions

What is this?

A publicly-editable pixel canvas tied to your current location. Think of it as MS Paint with a brush the size of a city block.

Who made it?

I did.

Why?

I decided to play with node.js and redis this week. Collaborative paint is a standard first app in node; I thought this would be a fun variation.

Why just Manhattan?

Because I go to Columbia, and can't fit a hi-res map of the entire universe in memory.
